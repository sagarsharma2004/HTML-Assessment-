MY FAVORITE MOVIES

  1. BHAGBAN
    • YEAR : 2003
    • MAIN ACTOR : AMITABH BACHCHAN
    • DESCRIPTION :

      The film tells the poignant story of Raj Malhotra (Amitabh Bachchan) and his wife Pooja (Hema Malini), an elderly couple who have devoted their lives to raising their four sons. As they grow older, they hope their children will take care of them in return. However, when Raj retires and the couple's financial situation changes, their sons, who are all well-settled, refuse to take responsibility for their parents together. Instead, the children propose that Raj and Pooja live separately with each of them for three months at a time. This arrangement causes great pain and suffering for the couple, as they feel neglected and disrespected by their own family. The film explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the bond between parents and children, highlighting the harsh reality of how elderly parents are sometimes treated by their own offspring.

  2. K.G.F CHAPTER 1
    • YEAR : 2018
    • MAIN ACTOR : YASH
    • DESCRIPTION :

      The story begins with the birth of Rocky in poverty-stricken Mumbai. As a young boy, he is determined to become rich and powerful, a promise he made to his dying mother. Rocky grows up to become a ruthless and ambitious gangster. He eventually arrives in Bangalore and, through a series of events, gets involved in the KGF, a notorious gold mine controlled by the tyrannical Suryavardhan and his son, Garuda. Rocky infiltrates the gold fields, where he witnesses the suffering of the workers under Garuda's oppressive rule. As he gains the trust of the workers and the people around him, Rocky plans a rebellion to overthrow Garuda. The film ends with a powerful climax, leaving audiences eager for the sequel, KGF Chapter 2.

  3. K.G.F CHAPTER 2
    • YEAR : 2022
    • MAIN ACTOR : YASH
    • DESCRIPTION :

      The movie picks up where the first part left off, with Rocky (played by Yash) rising to power as the overlord of the Kolar Gold Fields (KGF) after defeating Garuda. In KGF Chapter 2, Rocky faces new challenges as he navigates political and criminal conflicts. He aims to consolidate his control over the gold mines while dealing with new adversaries, including Adheera (played by Sanjay Dutt), a formidable foe with his own ambitions for the gold fields. The film explores themes of power, ambition, and revenge, with a backdrop of intense action sequences and dramatic confrontations. As Rocky seeks to establish his empire, he must confront forces both within and outside the criminal underworld, including the Indian government, which views him as a significant threat.

  4. BAHUBALI: The Beginning
    • YEAR : 2015
    • MAIN ACTOR : PRABHAS
    • DESCRIPTION :

      This film introduces the kingdom of Mahishmati and tells the story of two brothers, Amarendra Baahubali and Bhallaladeva, who vie for the throne. The story is centered around the journey of a young man, Shivudu (later revealed to be Mahendra Baahubali), who discovers his royal heritage and sets out to reclaim his kingdom from the tyrant Bhallaladeva. The movie ends on a cliffhanger with the question of why Kattappa, a loyal servant, killed Baahubali.

  5. BAHUBALI: The Conclusion
    • YEAR : 2017
    • MAIN ACTOR : PRABHAS
    • DESCRIPTION :

      This sequel answers the cliffhanger and continues the story, explaining the events that led to Amarendra Baahubali's death. It follows Mahendra Baahubali's quest to avenge his father's death and free the kingdom from Bhallaladeva's tyranny. The film delves into themes of loyalty, honor, and sacrifice, culminating in an epic battle for justice.
